• Luquillo is the beach day trip San Juan locals do when they want something a little further out but still easy to reach. Balneario de Luquillo has calm water, palm trees, and that classic northeast coast feel. Right next to it are the kioskos — a row of open-air spots where you grab alcapurrias, bacalaitos, and fresh seafood without leaving the sand. Go on a weekday if you want it quiet, or lean into the crowd on a Sunday. Either way, pack sunscreen and come hungry. Click here for directions: [🧭](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Balneario+de+Luquillo/@18.3725,-65.7165,15z)
