• Guavate is the lechonera strip on PR-184 in Cayey, and it's where Puerto Ricans go when they want a real Sunday lunch. You'll drive past spot after spot with whole pigs roasting on spits, mofongo on the menu, and live music spilling out onto the road. It's loud, casual, and very much a local thing — not a polished tourist stop. Get there hungry, pick a lechonera that looks busy, and don't skip the morcilla.
