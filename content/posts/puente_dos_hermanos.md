• Puente Dos Hermanos connects Condado to Miramar over the Laguna del Condado, and it's one of those spots that feels very San Juan once you're there. During the day you'll see people paddleboarding, kayaking, and jogging along the water with the city skyline behind them. At night the bridge lights up and the whole lagoon gets this calm, almost cinematic vibe. It's not a full-day trip — more like a sunset walk or a quick paddle session before dinner — but locals treat it like their backyard.
