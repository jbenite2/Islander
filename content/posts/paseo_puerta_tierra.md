• The Paseo Puerta de Tierra is the bike and running path that runs along the coast between Old San Juan and Condado. On weekends you'll see families on bikes, people jogging, and groups just walking the full route with the ocean on one side and the city on the other. It's flat, shaded in parts, and one of the easiest ways to see a stretch of San Juan without dealing with traffic. If you're staying anywhere near the capital, this is where locals actually get their steps in.
